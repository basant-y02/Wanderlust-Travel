About Wanderlust Travel

Our story, mission, and the team behind your unforgettable journeys

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by passionate travelers, Wanderlust Travel began as a small boutique agency with a big dream.

What started as a shared love for exploration between two friends has grown into a premier travel company serving thousands of clients worldwide. Our founders, Sarah and Michael, met while backpacking through Southeast Asia and realized they could help others experience the transformative power of travel.

Today, we're proud to have a team of over 50 travel experts who share our passion for creating meaningful, personalized travel experiences.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable extraordinary travel experiences that create lasting memories.

We believe travel has the power to change lives, broaden perspectives, and connect people across cultures. Our mission is to make these transformative experiences accessible to everyone through:

  • Personalized trip planning
  • Expert local knowledge
  • Sustainable travel practices
  • Exceptional customer service

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Passion for Travel

We live and breathe travel, and we're committed to sharing that passion with our clients through every journey we plan.

Personalized Service

Every traveler is unique, and we tailor each experience to match your individual preferences and dreams.

Sustainable Tourism

We're committed to responsible travel that benefits local communities and preserves our planet for future generations.
